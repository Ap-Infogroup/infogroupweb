What is Advance Market Maker?

Advance Market Maker is a three-month program suitable for all students and professionals who wish to specialize in the stock market trading (Equity, Equity Derivative, Currency & Commodity Markets) and technical analysis.

All those who have done MBA in Finance, Marketing and even HR or any other field can do this specialization course in share market as it is the most promising sector in the financial market. The module is based on theory as well as practical. The course includes capital, derivatives, options, commodity, and currency market operations in the broking industry, and technical analysis and option strategy in financial institutions and banks. This is a short-term course for three-months in the financial market as a part of BFSI segment.
